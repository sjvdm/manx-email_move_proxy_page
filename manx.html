A New Chapter for Manx.net Email.

 

Manx Telecom has subsidised Manx.net for over 25 years. Email services now require specialist management and strong protection against rising security risks. Junara already provide the platform behind Manx.net and will now take full responsibility for service and support. This ensures continuity for customers.

 

What you need to do

  • Register with Junara between 21 October 2025 and Tuesday 20 January 2026 to keep your @manx.net address.
  • Plans: £65 annually (discounted from £78) or £6.50 monthly.
  • Payment options: Register now and pay later (by Tue 20 Jan 2026), or pre pay during sign up.
  • Subscription begins Wednesday 21 January 2026.

 

If you don't register and pay

  • From Wed 21 January 2026 – your account will be suspended.
  • Grace period: Until Mon 20 April 2026 to reactivate.
  • Permanent deletion: Tue 21 April 2026 if not reactivated.

 

Support during the transition

  • Junara will provide technical and billing support after you register.
  • Manx Telecom will continue to help with general questions during the transition.
  • A chatbot on this page will answer common questions from 21 October 2025.

 

Security note

We will not send links in emails about this change. We will email your @manx.net address asking you to review the instructions here at www.manx.net. Please type the address into your browser and follow the steps.

 

Timeline at a glance

  • Tue 21 Oct 2025 – Registration opens
  • Tue 20 Jan 2026 – Registration & payment deadline
  • Wed 21 Jan 2026 – Suspension begins for unregistered/unpaid accounts; subscription commences
  • Mon 20 Apr 2026 – End of grace period
  • Tue 21 Apr 2026 – Permanent deletion for non-reactivated accounts

 

Thank you

We’re proud to have supported Manx.net for over 25 years. This transition ensures your email continues under specialist management while Manx Telecom focuses on the Island’s connectivity future.
