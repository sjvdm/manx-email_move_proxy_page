A New Chapter for Manx.net Email.

 

From 30th September 2025, the Manx.net email service will be moving to Junara, a specialist email provider who will take full ownership and management of the service.

 

What’s Changing?

  • For over 20 years, Manx Telecom has provided @manx.net email addresses free of charge to the Isle of Man community.
  • From 30th September 2025, Junara will take over full ownership and customer support for the Manx.net email service.
  • This ensures uninterrupted service, managed by an email specialist with robust security and infrastructure.

 

What You Need to Do?

  • To keep your @manx.net email address, you must register directly with Junara between 30th September and 4th November 2025.
  • Existing users will receive an exclusive £14 discount – the first year will cost just £65.
  • If you do not register by 4th November 2025, your account will be suspended from 5th November 2025.
  • A grace period until 23rd February 2026 allows reactivation if you miss the deadline.
